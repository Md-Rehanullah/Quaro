Frequently Asked Questions

How does anonymity work?

We don't collect or store any personal information. Questions and answers are posted without any identifying information attached.

How is my data stored?

All questions and answers are stored locally in your browser. No data is sent to external servers for the basic functionality.

How do you handle inappropriate content?

We rely on community reporting. Users can report inappropriate content, which is then reviewed by our moderation team.

Is the platform mobile-friendly?

Yes! AnonQ&A is designed to work seamlessly across all devices, from smartphones to desktop computers.
